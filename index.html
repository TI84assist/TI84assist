Sierpinski Triangle

Like the cool triangle image? Click on the image to load the program onto your calculator

The Sierpinksi Triangle is an instance of self similar sets, mathematically generated patterns that are reproducible at any magnification or reduction. It is named after the Polish mathematician Wacław Sierpiński, but appeared as a decorative pattern many centuries prior to the work of Sierpiński. The method demonstrated in the image on the left uses the chaos game, and is an example of an iterated function system. (Wikipedia)
